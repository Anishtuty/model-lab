Identification Details

  • Scientific Name: Naja naja
  • Venom Type: Neurotoxic
  • Venom Potency: High (LD50: 0.29 mg/kg)
  • Geographic Region: South Asia
  • Antivenom: Polyvalent Antivenom Available
  • Fatality Rate: 10-20% without treatment

Immediate Medical Recommendations

  • THIS IS A MEDICAL EMERGENCY - Seek help immediately
  • Keep patient calm and immobilize affected limb
  • Do not wash bite area (venom samples help ID antivenom)
  • Remove constrictive items (watches, rings, etc.)
  • Transport to hospital with ventilator support capability
  • Antivenom required: Polyvalent or Monovalent anti-cobra
  • Monitor respiratory function - may require ventilation
