## 📋 Overview

VenomAI Pro is an advanced web application that uses AI/ML to identify snake species from images and assess snakebite severity from bite mark photos. The system aims to reduce snakebite mortality by providing rapid, accurate diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

> ⚠️ **Important Disclaimer**: This application is for informational purposes only. Always seek immediate medical attention for snakebites. The AI analysis is a decision support tool, not a replacement for professional medical advice.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Snakebites cause severe health risks and deaths due to delays in identifying snake species and bite severity. Traditional diagnosis requires expert herpetologists who may not be available in remote areas or during emergencies.

### Our Solution:
- **Real-time Analysis**: Identify snake species within seconds
- **Severity Assessment**: Analyze bite marks for venom severity
- **Comprehensive Database**: 500+ snake species information
- **Treatment Guidance**: Immediate medical recommendations
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Works on smartphones with camera integration

## ✨ Features

### 🐍 **Snake Species Identification**
- Identify 500+ snake species from images
- 95% accuracy in species identification
- Detailed species information (venom type, habitat, antivenom)

### 🩺 **Bite Severity Assessment**
- Analyze bite marks for venom severity
- Bite verification to distinguish from other injuries
- Three severity levels: High, Medium, Low

### 📱 **Mobile Camera Integration**
- Direct camera access from mobile devices
- Real-time image capture
- Drag & drop image upload
- Supports JPG, PNG, JPEG up to 10MB

### 🗄️ **Comprehensive Database**
- 500+ snake species with detailed information
- Filter by venom level, region, or type
- Complete profiles with:
  - Scientific names
  - Venom type and potency (LD50 values)
  - Geographic distribution
  - Antivenom availability
  - Fatality rates
  - Habitat information

### 🚑 **Emergency Features**
- Step-by-step first aid guide
- Global antivenom database
- Hospital locator (integration ready)
- Emergency contact information
- Treatment timeline estimates

### 🎨 **User-Friendly Interface**
- Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Tabbed interface for different analysis types
- Visual severity indicators
- Interactive snake cards
- Modal for detailed snake information
